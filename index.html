Project Overview

The Problem

Utility companies often rely on manual inspections or specialized surveys to monitor vegetation encroachment on transmission lines. These methods can be labor-intensive, costly, and reactive rather than preventative.

  • Vegetation-related outages can be costly annually
  • Manual inspections limited by terrain and weather
  • Reactive maintenance increases wildfire risk

Our Solution

A vehicle-mounted monitoring prototype that captures geotagged imagery during routine operations, processes data through an AI analytics pipeline, and visualizes vegetation risk on a dashboard.

  • Scalable: convert existing fleet vehicles into data collectors
  • Automated capture with minimal operator interaction
  • Risk scoring to prioritize maintenance planning
